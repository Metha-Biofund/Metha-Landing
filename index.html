About Metha Biofund

Our Goal is to help extend
lifespan and healthspan.

Through our platform we try to create a decentralized ecosistem to give researchers the oportunity to change the world.

We will attract contributions by rewarding them with METHA tokens, an ERC20 token on the ETHEREUM blockchain.

This new currency will be the new standard for the longevity financial ecosistem.

The Problem

Conflict of interest

Many research institutions, specially in the field of preventive medicine and aging, are left unfunded.

They fall into a category, where they are not interesting nor profitable for governments or Big Pharma to fund.

This is where Metha Biofund appears to align interests through a revolutionary financial system for Longevity Research.

Power to the people.

The Metha Biofund Community

The union of research institutions and contributors will be at the core of the project.

We aim to align interests by rewarding contributions with metha tokens, the currency of Aging.

Power of Blockchain

Transparent Smart Contracts

Our decentralized ecosistem will provide with full transparent decision making and fund distribution.

Popular projects among the community will be voted on chain by auditors and be funded gradually to completion.

Contributors will be rewarded with their tokens, the new currency used by research institutions adhering to the program.
